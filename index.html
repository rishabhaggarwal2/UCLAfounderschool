About

UCLA Founder's School is a one-day conference covering various topics in entrepreneurship. We hope to bring together the most innvoative and brilliant students, speakers, and companies in Los Angeles.

Our theme for this year is Inspiration. We want to inspire young founders to create their own ventures, by offering small-size sessions and networking opportunities with the best speakers in tech.
